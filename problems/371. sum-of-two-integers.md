## 371.两整数之和

<p><strong>示例 1:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>输入: </strong>a = 1, b = 2
<strong>输出: </strong>3
</pre>

<p><strong>示例 2:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>输入: </strong>a = -2, b = 3
<strong>输出: </strong>1</pre>
